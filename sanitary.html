Ujjwal Sr. Sec. School, Bawania

Water and Sanitary Conditions

  • Safe and purified drinking water is available for all students and staff.
  • RO water purification systems are installed and maintained regularly.
  • Separate toilets for boys and girls on each floor.
  • Toilets for staff and differently-abled students are available.
  • Toilets are cleaned and sanitized multiple times daily.
  • Handwashing facilities with soap are available in all wash areas.
  • Waste water is drained properly through a covered drainage system.
  • Periodic hygiene and sanitation inspections are conducted by the school.
  • Proper disposal of solid and liquid waste is ensured.
